About us

Founded in 2024, Sweet Babe Inc. is a narrative development and consultation studio based in the internet. Our mission is to give what the market desires, maintaining what has already been working in the video games industry since Tetris. We aim to make games more engaging, more fun, and activate more monkey neurons, for everyone.

Services

We believe you need people with a functioning brain to solve non-problems. Sweet Babe Inc. provides narrative consultation at any stage of development, boasting a dude or more with self-vetted being-a-normal-gamer industry experience to best bring your story to life.
